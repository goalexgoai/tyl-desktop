The problem

Why not just start a group chat?

Group texts feel mass. Text Your List keeps every conversation one-on-one.

Group chats feel mass

When people get a group text, they know they're one of many. Response rates drop. A private text from your number gets read.

Everyone's replies go to everyone

One "reply all" from an oversharer derails the whole thread. Private texts keep conversations one-on-one.

You can't personalize a group text

With Text Your List, every message can say their name, reference something specific, or include a custom link — automatically.
