Text Your List

Let's get you set up

Grant system access to Text Your List.

1
Allow access to Messages

macOS will ask if Text Your List can control Messages.app. Click OK on the prompt that appears.

2
Grant Full Disk Access

Allow Text Your List to discern between iPhone and Android users. Apple requires you to grant this permission manually in System Settings.

Click "Open System Settings," find Text Your List (or Electron) in the Full Disk Access list and toggle it on.
Don't see it? Click the + button in System Settings and add Text Your List from your Applications folder.
