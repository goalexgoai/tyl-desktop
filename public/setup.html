Text Your List

Let's get you set up

Text Your List needs two permissions to route messages correctly — iPhone contacts go as iMessage, Android contacts go as SMS. Grant both now so you're never interrupted mid-send.

1
Allow access to Messages

macOS will ask if Text Your List can control Messages.app. Click OK on the system prompt.

2
Grant Full Disk Access

This allows Text Your List to read your Messages history to detect whether each contact is on iPhone or Android. Apple requires you to grant this manually in System Settings.

Click "Open System Settings," add Text Your List to the Full Disk Access list, then click "Check access."
