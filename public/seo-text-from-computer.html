Send texts from your computer — Mac or Windows

Managing a contact list from your phone is painful. Typing long messages on a small keyboard, switching between apps, losing your place — it slows everything down. Text Your List lets you send SMS from your Mac or Windows computer using your real phone number.

Why sending from your computer is better

A full keyboard makes writing faster and editing easier. You can manage your contact list in a spreadsheet, copy it in, and send to hundreds of people without touching your phone. You can queue up sends, set a pace, and walk away while messages go out.

Everything still goes through your real phone number. Replies come back to your phone's messages app. No new number, no carrier approval, no shortcode. It looks and feels like a personal text — because it routes through your phone.

How the companion app works

Text Your List uses a lightweight companion app that runs on your computer. It connects to the Text Your List dashboard and picks up queued messages, then routes them through your phone's messaging service. Your computer and phone both need to be on and connected while messages are sending.

  • 1 Mac + iPhone: messages route through the Mac Messages app using Apple's SMS relay feature.
  • 2 Mac + Android: messages route through Android Messages for Web, connected via QR code.
  • 3 Windows + Android: same as above — Windows companion uses Android Messages for Web.

Getting started

Sign up free, download the companion for your system, and you're sending in about five minutes. No carrier fees, no shortcode setup, no contract. The free plan includes 50 texts per month, with paid plans for higher volumes.
