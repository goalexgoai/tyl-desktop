Send personalized texts from a CSV file

If your contacts live in Excel, Google Sheets, or a CRM export, you're one upload away from sending each of them a personal text. Text Your List reads your CSV, maps your columns, and sends every person their own individual message from your real phone number.

The CSV workflow

Export your contacts from wherever you manage them — your CRM, a Google Sheet, a spreadsheet you've maintained for years. As long as it has a name column and a phone number column, you're ready to go.

  • 1 Export a CSV from your CRM, Google Sheets, or Excel with at minimum a name and phone number column.
  • 2 Upload the CSV to Text Your List and map your columns — tell it which column is the phone number, which is the first name.
  • 3 Write your message using merge fields like {first_name} or any other column from your spreadsheet.
  • 4 Preview how the message will look for a few contacts, then send.
  • 5 Each person receives their own private text from your real number. No group thread. No shared sender.

Who benefits from the CSV workflow

Anyone who already manages their contacts in a spreadsheet or CRM. Real estate agents with a sphere of influence list. Coaches with a roster. Church coordinators with a membership directory. Small business owners with a customer list. If your contacts are in a spreadsheet, Text Your List is built for you.

You can also personalize beyond just the name. Any column in your CSV can become a merge field. Reference a property address, a purchase date, a team position — whatever you have in your data, you can put in the message.

Works on Mac and Windows

Text Your List routes messages through a companion app on your computer that connects to your phone. iPhone users need a Mac. Android users can use Mac or Windows. There's a free plan to get started, with paid plans for higher volumes.
