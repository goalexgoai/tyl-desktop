Personal texting for churches and small religious groups

Your congregation doesn't want to feel like a mailing list. Text Your List lets pastors, coordinators, ministry leaders, youth group directors, and small group leaders send personal texts to every member — from their own real phone number, one at a time.

The problem with group texts for churches and small groups

When you send a group text, every reply goes back to everyone. One person's question, one "who is this?" — it lands in everyone's inbox. People stop engaging because it feels noisy and impersonal. The bigger the group, the worse it gets.

Mass SMS platforms fix the group thread problem but create a new one: messages arrive from a shortcode nobody recognizes. Response rates drop because it looks like marketing spam, not a personal message from their pastor, coordinator, or group leader.

Text Your List is built for groups where the relationship is personal but the list is too large to text one by one — a congregation of 80, a youth group of 40, a small group network, a ministry team. The people on your list already know you. They just need to feel that way when they receive your message.

How it works for religious and community groups

Every message goes out from your real phone number — the number your members already have saved. It looks like a personal text because it is one. Each person gets their own private message with their name, not a group blast. Replies come back to your regular messages app, one conversation at a time.

Upload your member list as a spreadsheet, write your message once, and Text Your List handles the rest. Use {first_name} to greet each person by name. Send service reminders, event invitations, prayer requests, class announcements, or personal check-in messages — all without the chaos of a group thread.

Works for ward coordinators, youth directors, small group leaders, nonprofit staff, and anyone who communicates regularly with a known group of people.

Works on Mac and Windows

A small companion app runs on your computer and routes messages through your phone. iPhone users pair with a Mac. Android users can use Mac or Windows. Setup takes about five minutes. No monthly shortcode fee. No carrier registration. No tech background required.

A note on responsible use

Text Your List is designed for groups where you have an existing relationship. Members of your congregation, students in your class, participants in your group — people who gave you their number and expect to hear from you. We don't recommend using this tool to contact people who haven't given you their number directly.
